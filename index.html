Welcome to pig-dice!

A dice game for the whole family....except Aunt Mary.

rules:

  • Players take turns rolling dice
  • The number you roll gets added to your turn score
  • If you roll a one, however, your turn score gets pushed back to 0, and your turn is over.
  • When you 'pass' to the next player any points you have in your turn score are saved forever and ever in your total score!
  • The first play that gets a total score of 100 or more WINS!

    • Have fun!
